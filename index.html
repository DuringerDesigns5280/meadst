WELCOME TO MEAD ST PROVISIONS

Mead St. Provisions is a locally owned and operated market, butcher, eatery and cocktail space. Our vision is to provide the freshest ingredients, CO products and prime cut meats in a trendy yet inviting space. Come enjoy a charcuterie board with a deliciously paired glass of wine and grab a steak to take home and grill for dinner! Do not forget to ask about our seasonings, sauces and dressings; we will take the work out of dinner for you. We offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options as well as a limited kid’s menu.
The market has everyday pantry essentials such as milk, eggs, snacks and paper products.
Brunch is offered every Saturday and Sunday from 10am-2pm.

PROVISIONS

Charcuterie
We swap out our charcuterie boards monthly with both local and international salumi, artisanal cheeses and provisions. Perfect snack for 2-3 adults.
Sandwiches
Our gourmet sandwiches are hand-sliced to order on our daily delivered fresh-baked breads. We make our mozzarella in-house as well our sauces; Giardiniera, roasted red peppers & pesto.
Cocktails
Our specialty cocktails are carefully selected and crafted with our wide range of clientele in mind from sweet to dry, simple to complex we have something that will please everyone.
Meats
We have partnered with 7x ranch for all wagyu beef and use local River Bear meats for all sausages. If there’s anything specific you’re looking for, please ask! We’re happy to accommodate.
